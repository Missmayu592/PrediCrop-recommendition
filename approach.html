Frequently Asked Questions

  • How this website helps farmers problems
    There are three major problems of farmers which are crop forecasting,fertilizer recommendations and weather forecasting and we solve those problems here. We also focus on the responsibility of farmers to see how they play a key role in the real world.
  • What are the advantages of this website?
    You get multiple facilities like how much fertilizer farmers used in their crop and what crop they grow in their particular region etc..
  • May work in different districts or states?
    Yes we understand your point which solves in different states of India and provides first rate facility.
  • How do you handle the technology trend?
    We use a forward-looking approach to generate a linear technology trend for each crop and each geography (county, state, national). Specifically, we combine linear trend estimates based on the past 20, 30, and 40 years (when available) to obtain a single best estimate of the trend. We also use a median regression approach, rather than least squares regression, so that the trend estimates are not overly affected by outlier years.
  • You can add international regions?
    In principle, yes, and we plan to add yield and production forecasts for Brazil and Argentina in time for the southern hemisphere summer of 2027/2028. We have excellent weather and satellite data for international yield modeling, but it is challenging to obtain long-term local yield data for building our statistical models. We expect to bridge this gap in data resources by applying CropProphet to downscale existing yield data, with additional constraints based on satellite crop analysis.
